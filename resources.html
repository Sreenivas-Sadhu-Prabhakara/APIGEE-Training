Instructor calibration notes

Apigee can enforce gateway controls, validate tokens, shape traffic, mediate payloads, and create operational evidence. It does not replace the authorization server, consent platform, fraud engine, backend authorization, or regulatory governance. The training explicitly teaches these boundaries so learners build with good judgment.
